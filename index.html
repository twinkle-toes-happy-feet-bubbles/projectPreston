    Life

    Tanner is a 17 year old recovering addict. These addictions caused him to burn many bridges, destroy many lives, drop out of school and get arrested. From not only using but also selling opioids, shrooms and everything in between to becoming a high school graduate and author is a truly incredible change.

  • The Mission

    The Mission

    His mission and goal is to change the lives of others in a positive way. Whether it is drugs, other addiction, depression, or even just not being where you want to be in life, his story can help.

  • Present

    Present

    Now, Tanner lives a pretty average life. He works fast food, has car trouble, and goes on road trips but also writes books, gives speeches, and aids those in need.
